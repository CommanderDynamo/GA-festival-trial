About Homowo

Homowo, meaning "hooting at hunger," is a major Ga-Dangme festival celebrating the overcoming of famine and the blessing of abundance.

Families, chiefs, and the diaspora come together to sprinkle **kpokpoi**, dance to traditional drums, and honor ancestors.
